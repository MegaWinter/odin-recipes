Congo Squares

Description

This is a dessert that my grandma taught me when I was younger, and it was such a formative part of my early years that I just had to pass this on.

This gooey chocolately cookie will be a hit with anyone that you bake for! Give it a try!

Ingredients

    Stuff you'll need for this

  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 1/4 cups packed light brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powderd
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  • Step 1:Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan. Set aside.
  • Step 2:In a large mixing bowl, combine butter or margarine and brown sugar; cool slightly. Beat in eggs, one at a time. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt; add to sugar mixture and mix well. Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
  • Step 3:Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. When almost cool, cut into 48 squares.
