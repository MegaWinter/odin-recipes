Zucchini Bread

Description

Getting all of your vegetables in the guise of a bread!

Guaranteed to make your toes curl

Ingredients

    Stuff you'll need for this

  • 3 to 4 cups grated fresh zucchini
  • 3/4 cup (170g) unsalted butter, melted, plus more for greasing the pans
  • 3 cups (390g) all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tsp cninnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  • Step 1:Drain the zucchini
  • Step 2:Prep the oven and pans
  • Step 3:Combine the dry ingredients
  • Step 4:Whisk the wet ingredients
  • Step 5:Mix the batter
  • Step 6:Bake the bread
